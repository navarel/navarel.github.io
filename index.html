DISCLAIMER

Due to their small population, the 3 Canadian Territories (Nunavut, the Northwest Territories, and the Yukon) do not have any major sports teams. However, recreational sports in these regions include hiking, snowshoeing, cross country skiing, and ice fishing. The website will not discuss these sporting options or regions.
